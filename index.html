About Tember

Past thoughts and present reflections as bridges to purposeful practice.

Add Tember to your phone

Tember revels in the creative tension between the past and one's purpose.

Quotes update every hour, inviting reflection and potential relevance to be found in the present moment. You're welcome to share an insight as prompted. Or, just enjoy this space and observe as others engage with it.

Tember is created and hosted by Anthony Burrow, who studies purpose and appreciates creating ways for people to sense more of it in their lives.

Add Tember to your phone
  1. Open tember.vercel.app in Safari on iPhone, or Chrome on Android.
  2. Tap Share, then Add to Home Screen.
  3. Keep the name Tember, then tap Add.
About reminders
  1. Email reminders can be saved in Settings.
  2. Device notifications require your browser or phone to allow them.
  3. You can cancel email reminders from Settings anytime.
